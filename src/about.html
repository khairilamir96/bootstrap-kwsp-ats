Organisation

The Employees Provident Fund (EPF) is one of the world’s oldest provident funds. Established in 1951, we help the Malaysian workforce to save for their retirement in accordance to the Employees Provident Fund Act 1991.Today, we at the EPF continue to refine our vision to not only stay relevant but to create a better retirement for all our members. This strengthens our commitment in safeguarding our members’ savings and increasing our dedication in providing excellent services.In tandem with ourmain vision in helping members achieve a better future, we have extended our mandate to include aiding national infrastructural development while safeguarding and growing members’ retirement savings.

Product & Services

EPF collects defined monthly contributions from employees through their employers. The contributions represent a certain percentage of theemployee’s income;supplemented by another percentage by the employers.EPF maintains records of contributions to monitor non-compliance. The accumulated contributions are then invested in various instruments allowed by the Act. EPF regularly advises employees on their savings via electronic channel, i.e. i-Akaun and Mobile Apps.As permitted by the Act, members may withdraw part of their savingsbefore the retirement age. The money can be used to purchase houses, pay for health treatments, pay for education or invest in unit trusts.For more information,please visit http://www.kwsp.gov.my
